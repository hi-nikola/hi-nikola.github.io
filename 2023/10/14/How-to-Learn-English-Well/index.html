How to Learn English Well
  1. What I’ve Done So Far
  2. My Future Steps

From now on, I will embark on English practice. First, I will draft blogs in English on my own and then refine them using ChatGPT. Each blog will revolve around a specific topic where I share my viewpoints.

Today’s topic centers on strategies for effective English learning and my upcoming plans.

What I’ve Done So Far

I’ve managed to memorize approximately 4,000 IELTS words. However, my challenge lies in employing them effectively in dialogues and sentences. Thus, I recognize the need to familiarize myself with their practical applications.

My Future Steps

  1. Read More: The adage “the more you read, the more you know” holds true. Reading extensively not only enriches my vocabulary but also demonstrates the ways in which words can be used.
  2. Watch More: I’ll invest time in viewing English vlogs, paying keen attention to both pronunciation and grammatical structures.
  3. Practice More: Emphasizing active usage, I plan to write frequently and converse with friends in English.
